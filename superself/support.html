Frequently Asked Questions

What is the philosophy behind SuperSelf?
SuperSelf is based on Wang Yangming's concept of "unity of knowledge and action" (知行合一). Affirmations can send spiritual energy to the universe, and tiny actions help coordinate your frequency with this spiritual energy, making manifestation faster and more effective.
How do I unlock the auto-affirm feature?
When your affirmation count exceeds 20, the auto-affirm feature automatically unlocks, allowing you to automate your daily affirmations for consistent practice.
When does the Tiny Action feature become available?
The Tiny Action feature unlocks when your affirmation count exceeds 50. Tiny actions help align your spiritual energy frequency and speed up manifestation by encouraging consistent small steps.
What is the optimal duration for tiny actions?
Tiny actions work best in 10-minute cycles. This duration is perfect for overcoming procrastination and building momentum. The short timeframe makes it easy to start and creates actionable steps toward your goals.
How often should I complete tiny actions?
Complete at least one tiny action per day. You don't need to set higher goals initially. Gradually developing the tiny action habit will accelerate your manifestation speed naturally.
How do tiny actions help with manifestation?
Tiny actions help align your spiritual energy frequency with the universe. By taking consistent small steps, you create momentum and demonstrate commitment to your goals, which accelerates the manifestation process.
