Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SuperSelf ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application SuperSelf (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

SuperSelf is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information or data from our users:

  • Local Storage Only: All manifestations, goals, and personal content you create within the app are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect usage statistics, device information, or any other personal data.
  • No User Accounts: The app does not require user registration or account creation.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information or data, there is no information to use, share, or process. Your data remains entirely on your device under your control.

Data Storage and Security

All your personal manifestations, goals, and app settings are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. No data is uploaded to our servers or transmitted over the internet. Your privacy is protected by keeping all data on your device.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to share or disclose. All your content remains private and local to your device.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we do not collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights and Data Management

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data at all times
  • No personal information is stored on our servers
  • When you delete the app, all associated data is automatically removed from your device
  • No additional steps are needed to delete your data since nothing is stored externally

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
