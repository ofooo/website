Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SuperSelf ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application SuperSelf (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

SuperSelf is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for app functionality:

  • Local and iCloud Storage: All manifestations, goals, affirmation counts, and personal content you create within the app are stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced to your personal iCloud account for backup and device synchronization purposes.
  • Affirmation Analytics: We store your monthly affirmation counts locally and in iCloud (if enabled) to track your progress and provide personalized insights.
  • No External Data Collection: We do not collect usage statistics, device information, or any other personal data beyond what you create within the app.
  • No User Accounts: The app does not require user registration or account creation with our services.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: SuperSelf does not integrate any third-party software development kits (SDKs) that could collect or transmit data to external services.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored in the app is used solely for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including manifestation tracking, goal setting, and progress monitoring
  • Progress Analytics: To calculate and display your monthly affirmation counts and personal achievement statistics
  • Data Continuity: To maintain your data across your devices through iCloud synchronization (when enabled)
  • No External Processing: Your data is never processed by external servers or third-party services

Data Tracking

We do not track user activities across apps or websites. No advertising identifiers or device fingerprints are used. SuperSelf operates without any external tracking mechanisms. The only data tracked is your voluntary affirmation activity within the app for your personal progress monitoring.

Data Storage and Security

Your personal manifestations, goals, affirmation counts, and app settings are stored using secure methods:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms with device-level encryption (File Protection API)
  • iCloud Synchronization: When enabled, your data is securely synchronized to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework, which provides end-to-end encryption
  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using Apple's security protocols
  • Sandboxed Storage: Data remains inaccessible to other apps through iOS sandbox protection
  • No External Servers: We do not operate any external servers or databases that store your information

Your privacy is protected by Apple's industry-leading security measures and our commitment to local data processing.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties. However, please note:

  • iCloud Storage: When you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Apple Services: Your data may be processed by Apple's iCloud infrastructure as part of the synchronization service
  • No Third-Party Access: We do not have access to your iCloud data and cannot retrieve or view your synchronized information
  • Legal Compliance: We may be required to disclose information if required by law, though we maintain no external databases to access

Your Rights and Data Management

You have complete control over your data:

  • Local Control: You can view, edit, and delete all data directly within the app
  • iCloud Management: You can enable or disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings
  • Data Export: You can export your data through the app's export features
  • Complete Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data; iCloud data can be deleted through your iCloud settings
  • iCloud Data Control: You can manage your iCloud data through your iOS device settings under iCloud storage management

Third-Party Services

SuperSelf integrates with the following Apple services:

  • iCloud/CloudKit: For optional data synchronization and backup across your devices
  • Apple's Privacy Policy: When using iCloud sync, your data is also subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • No Other Third Parties: We do not integrate with any other third-party services or analytics platforms

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we do not collect information from children under 13.

GDPR Compliance and Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: locally stored data (via app interface)
  • Delete: all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: export your data through the app's export features
  • Rectification: modify your data directly within the app

As no external data exists and all information remains on your device, no formal data subject request process is required. You maintain complete control over your data at all times.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
